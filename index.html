About

I am Robert Sory Jr. I build data pipelines and BI dashboards that answer real finance questions for real people. My workflow is simple and fast: Sheets or CSVs flow into Python for cleaning, then into BigQuery for storage, then into Looker Studio for interactive reporting. I package this so small businesses and teams can log in and get answers without touching code.

Core skills include Python, Pandas, SQL, BigQuery, Looker Studio, GitHub and automation. I focus on clean schema design, repeatable data quality checks and accessible UI. My finance tracker is a reusable system that adapts to messy exports and produces clear profit, spend and forecasting views. I can deploy to your Google environment or host a private instance and keep your data safe.

Keywords for search: data analyst, BI developer, Python, SQL, BigQuery, Looker Studio, data pipeline, ETL, dashboard, finance analytics, small business reporting, accessibility.
