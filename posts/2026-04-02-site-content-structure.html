Site content structure prepared for export-driven updates

Published 2026-04-02

This update establishes the first external posts source for the portfolio site. The homepage can now read recent posts from posts/posts.json instead of requiring every post mention to be hardcoded into index.html.

The goal is simple: future export workflows should be able to add a post file, update the posts index, and let the homepage surface the latest entry without restructuring the rest of the site.
