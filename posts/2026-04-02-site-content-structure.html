Site content structure prepared for export-driven updates

This update established the first external posts source for the portfolio site so new public-safe entries can be surfaced without hardcoding every mention into the homepage.

Why it matters
This created the content contract that lets Luna update the public site at scale later.

What changed

The homepage gained a feed path based on posts/posts.json. That separated the latest-posts surface from hand-edited homepage content and created the first durable outward publication contract.

Why it matters

Export-driven updates need a stable target. By giving the homepage an external posts source, future public-safe exports can add content by updating files rather than editing the homepage structure every time.
